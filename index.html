Who Can Apply?

This is a short film competition and hence ONLY Short Films should be submitted NO DOCUMENTARIES

DURATION: The films shall not be more than 40 Min, including the beginning and end credits. Films exceeding this time limit are liable to be rejected. The minimum length should be 30 seconds.

The short films may be in their original language, but MUST be subtitled in ENGLISH.

FORMAT: The films may be shot in MPEG4 or AVI format. The recommended ratios are: 16:9 / 16:9.

COPYRIGHT: The Film must not contain any copyrighted work(s) belonging to third parties unless (1) Participants have a license to use such works in the Film; or (2) the use participants’ Film makes of such works is a fair one under the copyright laws. Participants will retain any licenses and provide them to organizers upon request.

How to Apply?

Online Entry

Entry Fee : The entry fee is non refundable.

For Indian filmmaker Regular Entry Fee – Rs.500/- & Late Fee – Rs. 1000/-

For foreign filmmakers Regular Entry Fee – US$50 or equivalent and Rs.2000/- Late Fee – US $ 75 or equivalent.

Entrants are required to submit Youtube link / DVDs (Tested / All Code) / region free only), Promos, Trailers and a separate email containing Directors Photograph, Bio-data, Filmography, Synopsis of the film, Stills of the Film, Posters, Pamphlets, etc.
